# Privacy Policy for ALWAYS Receptionist

**Last Updated: November 3, 2025**

## Introduction

ALWAYS Receptionist ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify app.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Store Information
When you install ALWAYS Receptionist, we collect:
- **Store domain** (e.g., yourstore.myshopify.com)
- **Store owner email address**
- **OAuth access tokens** (to access your product and inventory data)
- **App configuration settings**

### 2. Product and Inventory Data
To enable our AI receptionist to answer customer questions, we access:
- **Product titles and descriptions**
- **Product availability and inventory levels**
- **Product prices**
- **Order information** (order numbers, status, customer details)

We only access this data when a customer calls and asks about products or orders. We do NOT store product or order data in our systems.

### 3. Call Data
When customers call your AI receptionist, we collect and store:
- **Call recordings** (audio)
- **Call transcripts** (text version of the conversation)
- **Call metadata** (date, time, duration, caller phone number)
- **Call summaries** (AI-generated summary of the conversation)
- **Customer questions and AI responses**

### 4. Technical Data
We automatically collect:
- **IP addresses** (for security and fraud prevention)
- **Browser type and version** (when you access the app dashboard)
- **Device information** (when you access the app dashboard)
- **Usage analytics** (how you interact with the app)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide AI Receptionist Services**
   - Answer customer calls with AI voice assistant
   - Respond to product inquiries using your Shopify data
   - Look up order status when customers request it
   - Generate call summaries and insights

2. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze call patterns to improve AI responses
   - Identify common customer questions
   - Enhance voice recognition accuracy
   - Debug technical issues

3. **Communication**
   - Send important service updates
   - Notify you of new features
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Send compliance-related notices

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws
   - Respond to legal requests and prevent fraud
   - Maintain audit trails for compliance purposes

## How We Share Your Information

### Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party services:

1. **VAPI.ai** (Voice AI Platform)
   - Purpose: Powers the voice AI assistant
   - Data shared: Call audio, transcripts, product information
   - Privacy policy: https://vapi.ai/privacy

2. **Shopify** (E-commerce Platform)
   - Purpose: Access your store data via Shopify API
   - Data shared: App installation status, webhook events
   - Privacy policy: https://shopify.com/legal/privacy

3. **Vercel** (Hosting Provider)
   - Purpose: Hosts our application infrastructure
   - Data shared: All data stored in our database
   - Privacy policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. **OpenAI** (AI Language Model)
   - Purpose: Powers the conversational AI
   - Data shared: Call transcripts, product information
   - Privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Use your data for advertising
- ❌ Share your data with competitors
- ❌ Use customer call data to train public AI models

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Call logs and transcripts | Indefinite (until deletion requested) | Provide call history and analytics |
| Store authentication tokens | Until app uninstall | Maintain app connection |
| VAPI configuration | Until app uninstall | Maintain assistant settings |
| GDPR request logs | Until app uninstall | Compliance audit trail |

### Automatic Deletion

Your data is automatically deleted when:
- You **uninstall the app** (all data deleted immediately)
- A **customer requests deletion** (their call data deleted within 48 hours)
- A **shop/redact webhook** is triggered (all data deleted within 48 hours)

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### GDPR Rights (European Union)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain data processing activities

### CCPA Rights (California)
- **Right to Know**: Know what data we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of data "sales" (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### How to Exercise Your Rights

1. **Access your data**: Visit the "GDPR Compliance" page in the app dashboard
2. **Request deletion**: Uninstall the app or email privacy@always-receptionist.com
3. **Customer data requests**: Use Shopify's GDPR tools (we'll respond automatically)
4. **Questions**: Email privacy@always-receptionist.com

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in transit**: All data encrypted with TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest**: Database encrypted with AES-256
- **Access controls**: Role-based access to sensitive data
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 for Shopify integration
- **API security**: Signature-based webhook verification

### Organizational Safeguards
- **Employee training**: Privacy and security training for all staff
- **Access logging**: All data access is logged and monitored
- **Incident response**: Documented breach notification procedures
- **Regular audits**: Quarterly security and privacy reviews

### Limitations
While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any breaches as required by law.
